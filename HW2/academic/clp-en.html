College of Law and Politics

Introduction

The colleges, including one department and three graduate institutes, originally belonged to the College of Social Science and Management. In August 2011, the College of Social Science and Management was renamed the College of Management, which was approved by the Ministry of Education. Afterwards, the College of Law and Politics set up.

Future development
