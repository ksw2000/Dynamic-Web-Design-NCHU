College of Agriculture and Natural Resources

About the College

The College aims to "cultivate modern youth with expertise in agricultural production science, natural resource management and conservation, and rural community development, as well as an integrated, innovative, and international outlook. The College's educational goals are "to develop sustainable and innovative agricultural science" and "to create a harmonious and safe natural environment". Students' core competencies are "innovation in agronomic knowledge and application of practical skills," "cross-domain integration and self-directed learning," "communication and teamwork," and "implementation of humanistic concern"; their basic qualities are "professional agronomic knowledge," "pragmatic and hard-working spirit," "concern for society and service to others," and "international perspective.

With a strong faculty and excellent equipment, the College has nurtured many excellent agricultural technology talents at home and abroad. Looking into the future, in addition to continuing to develop the existing characteristics and cultivate modern agricultural science and technology talents, the Academy will also strengthen the teaching and research of natural resources, with biotechnology, natural resource conservation and sustainable agriculture as the focus of development, in order to maintain human health and ecological environment harmony and benefit the community.

These are the key points of development for biotechnology, natural resource conservation and sustainable agriculture.

In order to ensure the quality of teaching and solid training of students in agriculture and natural resources, all departments and programs have made great progress in diversification, networking and internationalization of teaching. In particular, many units have experience in cooperating with foreign schools in teaching internationalization.
